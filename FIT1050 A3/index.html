Frequently Asked Questions

  • The main philosophy of Bent on Food is "local is always best." They emphasize using locally produced food rather than buying organic food that has traveled long distances.

  • Bent on Food’s menu promotes products available for sale in the store, with an ever-changing chef’s selection board that offers innovative ways to create meals from the wide range of products available.

  • The owner of Bent on Food is Donna Carrier, who has 18 years of experience in the wine industry and an Associate Diploma in Wine Marketing from Roseworthy College.

  • Bent on Food offers a variety of services and experiences, including local music nights, catering services, and a hamper service that delivers anywhere in Australia.

  • Bent on Food integrates sustainability into their operations by prioritizing locally sourced produce over organic products that have to be transported from afar.
